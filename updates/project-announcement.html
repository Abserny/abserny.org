Project Announcement

Abserny (أبصِرني) is officially announced as a graduation project focused on creating accessible technology for visually impaired Arabic-speaking users.

Project Vision

Our goal is to provide a privacy-first, offline object detection system that empowers visually impaired users through:

  • Voice-activated controls for hands-free operation
  • Natural Arabic language interaction
  • Complete offline functionality for privacy
  • Accessible design from the ground up

Follow our progress through this documentation site!
